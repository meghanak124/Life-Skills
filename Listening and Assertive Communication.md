# Listening and Active Communication
## Question 1
What are the steps/strategies to do Active Listening? (Minimum 6 points)
- Avoid getting distracted by your own thoughts
- focus on speaker nad topic
- donot interrupt
- let speaker finish and then reply
- use door openers to show interest
- take notes for important conversations

## Question 2
According to Fisher's model, what are the key points of Reflective Listening? (Write in your own words, use simple English)
- Be Attentive : Focus on what speaker is talking.
- Respond : Ask questions or acknowledge to ensure you understand the speaker's message.
- Physical Gestures : Show you attention with physical gestures as well.

## Question 3

- Loosing attention with inner thoughts

- careless physical gestures does not show interest
