# Grit and Growth Mindset

## Question 1
#### Paraphrase (summarize) the video in a few (1 or 2) lines. Use your own words.
- Grit is the ability to stay passionate and work hard toward long-term goals over time, even when progress is slow or setbacks occur.
- It shows that consistent effort matter more than talent alone for achieving success.
- Not giving up until reaching the milestone.

## Question 2
#### Paraphrase (summarize) the video in a few (1 or 2) lines in your own words.
- The growth mindset teaches that intelligence and skills aren’t fixed — people can improve through practice and effort.
- When we view challenges as opportunities to learn rather than obstacles, we grow our abilities over time.
- Believing that "Failure is the stepping stone to success"

## Question 3
#### What is the Internal Locus of Control? What is the key point in the video?
- The Internal Locus of Control is the belief that your own actions and decisions decide your outcomes in life — not luck, other people, or external circumstances.
- The key point is that when you believe you control your success, you stay more motivated, take responsibility, and be consistent through difficulties rather than feeling powerless.
- Believe in actions rather than other distractions like luck, fate, partiality etc
