# Prevention of Sexual Harassment
## Question 1
- What kinds of behaviour cause sexual harassment?
1. Physical contact and advances.
2. A demand or request for sexual favours.
3. Making sexually coloured remarks or words.
4. Showing sexually offensive content.
5. Any other unwelcome physical, verbal, or non-verbal conduct of a sexual intentions.

## Question 2
- What would you do in case you face or witness any incident or repeated incidents of such behaviour?
1. Inform the harasser that the abusive behavior is unwelcome and ask them to stop immediately.
2. Do keep a record of incidents (date, times, locations, possible witnesses, what happened, your response, etc)
3. If, after asking the harasser to stop the abusive behavior, the harassment continues, report the incident in writing to the  ICC (Internal Complaint Committee) member.
4. I may also have the right to file a charge of assault with the police.
5. Depending on the situation, Let the people know about the issue. Not responding may lead to entension of harrasment.
