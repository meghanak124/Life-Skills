# LEARNING PROCESS
## Question1
What is the Feynman Technique? Explain in 1 line.
Feynman Technique is named after Feynman. The Feynman Technique is a learning method where you understand a concept by explaining it in simple words as if teaching a beginner.

## Question3
What are active and diffused modes of thinking?
- Active Mode: Concentrated thinking used while solving problems or learning new concepts.
- Diffused Mode: Relaxed thinking that helps make connections when you step away from focused work.

## Question4
